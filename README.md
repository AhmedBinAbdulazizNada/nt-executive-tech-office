# NT Executive Technology Office

Welcome to the Executive Technology Office.

This repository acts as the company’s central intelligence system for technology research, market observation, innovation discovery, and strategic decision support.

The goal is simple:

Turn global technology movement into practical company decisions.

---

## Purpose

Technology moves faster than traditional planning.

This repository exists to help leadership continuously answer:

* What changed?
* What matters?
* What should we adopt?
* What should we ignore?
* Where should we invest?
